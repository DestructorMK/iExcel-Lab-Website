CSC 791
Multimodal Data Analysis
Spring · 2026
Multimodal data analysis is an advanced course that explores how meaning is made across data spanning multiple modalities such as language, visuals, sound, gesture, and digital media. Students engage with leading theories and hands-on methods for analyzing complex, real-world data from social media, videos, scientific images, and interactive platforms.
CSC 554/791
Human Computer Interface
Fall · 2025
Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) is the study of principles and methods to build effective interactions between computing devices and users. This course introduces theory and practice of developing user interfaces with a particular focus on human-centered methods. Students participate in group projects to design, implement, and evaluate user interfaces.
CSC 442
Introduction to Data Science
Spring · 2025
Data science has become increasingly important in nearly every industry sector and academic field. This course provides an overview of several foundational topics in data science, including the theory and algorithms underlying these techniques, combining computer science, statistics, and mathematics to extract knowledge from data.

Recommendation Letters

You should have either taken a class with me or worked with me on a project. Note the difference between a letter of reference and a strong letter of reference, which needs specific evidence.

Please email the following:

  1. A picture of you, to help me remember our interactions.
  2. Your UnityID — I can use this to look up your academic record.
  3. A list of courses you've taken and grades received. An unofficial transcript is fine; please highlight courses taken with me.
  4. A current copy of your resume.
  5. Any required forms.
  6. A personal statement: why your background is a good match for this opportunity, any exceptional work done in my classes, and specific evidence of your characteristics such as motivation, analytical thought, technical knowledge, creativity, research skills, teamwork, or leadership.

After we've agreed that I will write you a letter, if you haven't heard from me, please contact me a week before the deadline.
